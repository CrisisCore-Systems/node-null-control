Version: v01

## Structure

```
[Question that challenges assumption or creates curiosity]
```

## Timing

- Duration: 1.5-3 seconds
- Placement: First line/frame

## Formula

```
"{{question_word}} {{unexpected_element}} {{topic_reference}}?"
```

## Variables

- `{{question_word}}`: Why / What / How / Did you know
- `{{unexpected_element}}`: The surprising or counterintuitive element
- `{{topic_reference}}`: Connection to the content topic

## Examples (structure only)

- "Why do [X] when [Y] exists?"
- "What happens when [common assumption] is wrong?"
- "How does [thing] actually work?"

## Guidelines

- Question must be genuinely interesting, not clickbait
- Answer should be delivered in the content
- Avoid misleading or deceptive questions
- Question should be relevant to actual content

## Anti-patterns (do NOT use)

- Questions with obvious answers
- Questions designed to frustrate
- Misleading questions that content doesn't answer
- Questions that mock or shame the viewer
