# Hook Template H2 — Statement Hook

Version: v01

## Structure

```
[Strong declarative statement that establishes authority or presents insight]
```

## Timing

- Duration: 1.5-3 seconds
- Placement: First line/frame

## Formula

```
"{{subject}} {{strong_verb}} {{insight_or_claim}}."
```

## Variables

- `{{subject}}`: The topic or actor
- `{{strong_verb}}`: Active, definitive verb
- `{{insight_or_claim}}`: The core message (must be supportable)

## Examples (structure only)

- "[Topic] works differently than you think."
- "The pattern behind [phenomenon] is [insight]."
- "[Common belief] is changing — here's what's happening."

## Guidelines

- Statement must be accurate and supportable
- Avoid hyperbole or exaggeration
- Confidence should match evidence level
- Statement should lead naturally into content

## Anti-patterns (do NOT use)

- Unverifiable claims
- Sensationalist statements
- Fear-mongering declarations
- Statements that shame or attack
