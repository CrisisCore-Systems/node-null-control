# Kill Log

Decision log for patterns and verticals that were killed.

See [analytics/schema.md](../schema.md) for kill criteria and [docs/OPERATIONS.md](../../docs/OPERATIONS.md) for the full workflow.

---

## Format

```markdown
## YYYY-WXX

### Killed: [Pattern/Vertical Name]

- **Reason**: [Below-baseline completion + save/share after 2 iterations | Policy risk | etc.]
- **Samples**: N valid samples
- **Metrics**: Completion X% (baseline Y%), Save+Share X% (baseline Y%)
- **Decision date**: YYYY-MM-DD
```
